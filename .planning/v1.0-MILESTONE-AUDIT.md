### Non-Critical Items (2 items across 2 areas)

**04-anomaly-detection**
- `GET /api/v1/anomalies/filter-options` endpoint is registered in the router but never called by the frontend. `AnomaliesPage.tsx` derives filter options client-side from the unfiltered `useAnomalies()` response. Endpoint works correctly but is dead. Could be used in a future optimization to avoid fetching all records just for filter population.
